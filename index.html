Our story.

Scientpad Analytics is a plug and play AI/ML driven marketing data science platform which automatically derives the known unknown and unknown insights of online users and helps marketers to reach out to their customers in a very micro-segmented way.



Three layers.

Scientpad I/O allows to connect various data sources for ML/AI layer and sends the data insights to various marketing engagement platforms for personalization

Scientpad ML layer analyzes all possible data points with an objective to maximize the business goal and generates the actionable insights

Scientpad AI layer automates the whole process and improves the insights through feedback loops (using reinforcement learnings)
