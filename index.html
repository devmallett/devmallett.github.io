Background

Dev Mallett is from Cleveland, OH with a bachelor’s degree in Mechanical Engineering.

In college, Dev was an involved leader who aided in a number of projects/initiatives across campus, ranging from social activism to prospective student outreach.

Dev was selected as the Class of 2017’s Commencement speaker as he displayed what it means to learn, lead and serve. Dev previously worked in Business Development/Sales Team at Popmenu, LLC.
